Scrambled Eggs

Description

This Scrambled Egg is a soft, fluffy and creamy flavourful breakfast food.

Odin Recipes has garnered successful stories that helped made this scrambled egg unique and forever cherished.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Milk, plant milk, or water
  • Olive oil or butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Beat your eggs and place them in a bowl, and whisk until the yolk and whites are combined.
  2. Add the milk or water, and whisk again! The beaten eggs should be even yellow color.
  3. Next, gently peheat the pan. Melt a little butter inside it.
  4. Finally, cook by pouring the mig exture and let it heat for a few seconds.
  5. Pull a rubber spatula across the bottom of the pan to form large, soft curds of scrambled eggs.
  6. Enjoy!
