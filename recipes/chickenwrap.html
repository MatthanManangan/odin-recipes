Crispy Chicken Wrap

Description

This Crispy Chicken Wrap is a great speedy lunch snack that is combined with cooked chicken

Odin Recipes has garnered successful stories that helped made this wrap unique and forever cherished.

Ingredients

  • Cooked Crispy Chicken
  • Flour Tortilla
  • Aloli Mayo Sauce or Caesar Garlic Sauce
  • Shredded Lettuce
  • 2x Tomato Slices
  • 2x Bacon (optional)
  • Shredded Cheese

Steps

  1. First, cook your chicken breast with oil then bring to a boil.
  2. Simmer, for 4-10 minutes. Thickness of breasts will determine how long you need to cook it.
  3. Grill your Tortilla Wrap by 10 seconds.
  4. Put a horizontal spurt of Aloli Mayo or Caesar Garlic Sauce in the middle of your Tortilla wrap.
  5. Put your these ingredients in order to the middle of your Tortilla Wrap
    • Shredded Lettuce
    • Shredded Cheese
    • (Optional) 2x Bacon
    • 2x Tomtao Slices
  6. Fold the left and right sides of Tortilla wrap onto the ingredients then fold the middle over your folded sides.
  7. Roll the middle until your reach the end
  8. Enjoy!
