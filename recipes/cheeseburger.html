Cheeseburger

Description

This Cheeseburger is a classic fast food resturant burger that is popularised within Western countries.

Odin Recipes has garnered successful stories that helped made this burger unique and forever cherished.

Ingredients

  • Burger Bun & Heel
  • 4-6oz Burger Meat
  • Bottle Ketchup
  • Bottle Mustard
  • 2x Pickles (optional)
  • 1x Cheese
  • 5g Diced Lettuce

Steps

  1. First, grill your favourite burger meat patty for your burger.
  2. Then, put a small bottle spurt of ketchup and mustard on your Burger Bun.
  3. (Optional) Add two slices of pickles for preference.
  4. Add your favourite slice of cheese.
  5. After you finish grilling your burger patty, place it on top of your cheese.
  6. Finally, adding diced lettuce on your Burger Heel.
  7. Enjoy!
